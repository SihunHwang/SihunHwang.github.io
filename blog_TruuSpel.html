TruuSpel

September 2024

Learning German and French, their written texts were a bit easier to read. I thought that if we slightly change the spelling of English, then it would annoy less people.

The following is my proposal 'TruuSpel' for reformed spelling.

Idea :

  • Create a bijective map $\phi \colon \{ \textrm{IPA} \} \to \{ \textrm{letters} \}$
  • Reformed Spelling = $\phi$(IPA(Origianl English))

Define $\phi$ as :
