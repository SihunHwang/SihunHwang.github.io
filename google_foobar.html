Google Foobar Challenge

Google Foobar challenge is a secret hiring process
to recruit top programmers and developers around the world.

Access to the challenge is by invitation only which is based on google search history.

The challenge consists of five levels with a total of 9 questions,
with the level of difficulty increasing at each level.
